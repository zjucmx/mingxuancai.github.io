About me

I am a prospective Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley. I will be working with Prof. Laura Waller at The Computational Imaging Lab. I graduated with honor from the department of Optical Science and Engineering in Zhejiang University. From 2020-2022, I had worked on Super-resolution Microscopy, supervised by Prof. Cuifang Kuang and worked at The Super-resolution Optical Microscopy Lab. My research interests lie in the computational imaging, optical imaging, microscopy, inverse problem, optimization, and deep learning. I am an enthusiast of swimming, hiking and everything related to optics! My name in Chinese is 蔡明轩.

I will be studying in Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at University of California, Berkeley in the fall of 2022!
